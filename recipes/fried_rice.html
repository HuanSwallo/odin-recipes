Fried Rice

Craving something quick to eat? Try out this easy to make fried rice recipe that can be cooked in 20 minutes!

All you need is a pan and a few ingredients to get started!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients together.
  2. Get a small saucepan and place your carrots covered with water. Turn to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in the peas and then drain in a colander immediately.
  3. Heat pan over high heat. Pour vegetable oil and then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic for about 30 seconds. Add eggs and stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Now stir in the cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss the rice. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve in a plate and enjoy!
